pub fn new(buf_capacity: usize, slice_capacity: usize) -> SliceBuf<T, M>
[src]

Creates a new slice buffer.

The resulting buffer can store at at most buf_capacity T's and slice_capacity T slices. However depending on the alignment of slices within the buffer, enqueueing a slice may fail if the available capacity of the buffer is split among the head and tail. To avoid this, allocate at least 2 x the minimum buffer capacity you need.
